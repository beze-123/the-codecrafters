### Go constant
Go constant can be used when a variable has a fixed and unchangeable value.
Constant name cannot be changed.
Constant names are written in capital letter.
It can be declared both in and outside a function.
      There are two types of constants  1. typed 
                                        2. untyped constants
Typed constant are declared with a known variable while untyped constant are declared with known /specific variable.
